Autonomous AI Stock Investor

StockMind is an AI agent that autonomously manages a virtual $100,000 stock portfolio using real market data. It thinks, researches, trades, and learns â€” all on its own, like a curious human investor.

How It Works

1. Natural Daily Rhythm

StockMind doesn't run on a fixed timer. It follows a human-like schedule â€” morning check with coffee, occasional midday glances, afternoon reviews before close, and evening research sessions. Sometimes it skips a session, just like a real person.

2. Market Analysis

Each session, the AI reviews its portfolio, checks market indices (S&P 500, Dow, NASDAQ), scans its watchlist prices, and recalls its recent trades and lessons learned. It sees real-time prices from Yahoo Finance.

3. AI Thinking

Claude (by Anthropic) powers StockMind's brain. It receives all the market context and thinks out loud â€” you can watch its reasoning in real-time on the dashboard. It develops opinions, spots patterns, and debates with itself about trades.

4. Research

When something catches its eye, StockMind digs deeper. It pulls company fundamentals (P/E ratio, revenue growth, debt-to-equity, free cash flow), technical indicators (RSI, moving averages, volatility), upcoming earnings dates, and recent news â€” then forms an opinion.

5. Trading

If the AI sees a genuine opportunity, it executes paper trades at real market prices with simulated slippage. Every trade is logged with the AI's reasoning. It can buy, sell, or decide to do nothing â€” most sessions are observational.

6. Memory

StockMind learns from experience. It can save lessons and strategy notes to its memory, which it reviews before each session. Over time, it builds up a knowledge base of what worked and what didn't.
